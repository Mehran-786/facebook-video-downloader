Facebook Video Downloader - Top FAQs

Q1. Is it free and legal to download Facebook videos?

Yes. The Facebook Video Downloader is free to use in your browser and does not require installation. Laws vary by country, so do not download copyrighted material for commercial use without the rightsholder’s permission.

Q2. Do I need to install any app or software?

No. This is an online video downloader that works in most modern browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox) on desktop and mobile without installing an app. Simply paste the Facebook video URL into the tool to start the download process.

Q3. How can I download Facebook videos on mobile?

Copy the video link from Facebook (Share → Copy Link), paste the URL into the downloader field, choose MP4 or MP3 and the desired quality, then tap Download. If Copy Link isn’t available, open the post in a new tab or use the Share menu to get the URL.

Q4. Can I download in Full HD (1080p, 4K) quality?

Yes. The downloader offers the highest quality available from the original post, which may include 1080p or 4K if the uploader provided those resolutions. Higher-quality files are larger and can take longer to download.

Q5. Can I download Facebook Reels, Stories, or Live videos?

Yes. Public Reels and Stories can be saved using the same process—copy the post URL, paste it into the tool, then download; Live videos are downloadable only after the broadcast ends and the post is available. If a Reel or Story is embedded or private, open it in a new tab to copy the URL or confirm you have permission.

Q6. Can I download private Facebook videos?

Only if you can already view the private video on Facebook. The downloader requires a URL that opens in your browser and does not bypass Facebook privacy settings or DRM, so obtain permission from the owner before saving private content.

Q7. Will the videos have a watermark?

No. We do not add watermarks to downloaded files; the saved video is the same content served by the original Facebook post. Note that some original uploads may include branding added by their creators.

Q8. Can I convert a Facebook video to MP3?

Yes. Choose the MP3 option to extract audio from a Facebook video, and select a bitrate if the tool offers one. The resulting MP3 is compatible with most players and devices.

Q9. Where are the downloaded videos saved?

Downloaded files go to your device’s default Downloads folder. On mobile, check the Downloads folder or your Photos/Gallery app; on desktop, look in the browser’s Downloads location or use the browser download history to locate the file.

Q10. Is my data safe and why do some videos fail to download?

No login is required to use the tool; we only process the URL and temporary technical data to complete downloads. Downloads can fail if the URL is incorrect, the post is private or deleted, or regional/temporary errors occur—verify the link and try again.
